Zheng Chen

I am a first-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Yulun Zhang. Previously, I received my B.E. degree from SJTU in 2022. My master’s advisor is Prof. Linghe Kong.

My research interest is computer vision, focusing on image restoration (e.g., super-resolution, deblurring).
